# The Ultimate Aesthetic Desk Setup for Back to School 2026

Welcome back to a new semester, besties! 💖 If there's one thing that guarantees a productive study session, it's having an aesthetic and cozy desk setup. 

## The Essentials

1. **Pastel Mechanical Keyboard**: A creamy, clicky keyboard in matcha green or pastel pink is an absolute must-have.
2. **Warm Ambient Lighting**: Ditch the harsh dorm lights. Get a sunset lamp or some warm fairy lights to set the mood.
3. **A Smart Battery App**: If your MacBook is permanently plugged into your monitor on your desk, you are secretly killing its battery. Use **CasYuk** to cap your charge at 80%. It runs silently in the background, keeping your battery healthy so you can unplug and take it to your 8 AM lecture tomorrow without worrying about it dying.

Your desk should be your sanctuary. Keep it cute, keep it clean, and protect your tech! ✨
