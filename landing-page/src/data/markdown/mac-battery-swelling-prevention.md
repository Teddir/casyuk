# Why Your MacBook Battery is Swelling (And How to Prevent It)

A swollen laptop battery isn't just annoying; it's a dangerous fire hazard. It happens when the lithium-ion cells produce excess gas due to internal chemical breakdown.

## The Causes of Swelling

1. **Age**: All batteries degrade over time, but usually, they just lose capacity, they don't swell.
2. **Chronic Overcharging**: Leaving a laptop at 100% 24/7.
3. **Overheating**: Using the laptop on a bed or couch where vents are blocked.

## The $250 Mistake

When a battery swells, it pushes against the trackpad (breaking it) and bends the chassis. Apple charges upwards of $250 to replace it out of warranty.
