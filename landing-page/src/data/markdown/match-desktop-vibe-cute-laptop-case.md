# How to Match Your Desktop Vibe with Your Cute Laptop Case

Aesthetics are everything. If you bought a gorgeous pastel lavender case for your MacBook, but your screen is cluttered with messy blue folders and ugly default widgets, the vibe is ruined. 🙅‍♀️

## Creating a Cohesive Theme

1. **Color Palette Matching**: Find the exact hex code of your laptop case and use it to create custom desktop folder icons. 
2. **The Right Apps**: Use apps that let you customize their appearance. 
3. **Neo-Brutalism meets Coquette**: Did you know the **CasYuk** battery app uses a super trendy Neo-Brutalist design? It looks incredibly aesthetic on a well-organized screen. Plus, you can customize the video alerts—so yes, you can make a cute Taylor Swift edit pop up when your battery is low! 🎀✨
