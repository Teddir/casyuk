# Studygram Essentials: How to Make Your Digital Workspace Aesthetic

If you're obsessed with the #Studygram aesthetic on TikTok and Instagram, you know that a cute physical desk is only half the battle. Your digital workspace needs to match the vibe. 🎀

## Customizing Your Mac

1. **Folder Icons**: Change those boring blue folders to cute PNG icons (like little clouds, coffee cups, or pastel hearts). 
2. **Minimalist Wallpapers**: A clean, neutral wallpaper with a built-in calendar reduces visual clutter.
3. **Widget Customization**: Use widgets for a pomodoro timer and aesthetic quotes.

## The Vibe Check for Notifications

Don't let ugly, stressful system popups ruin your flow. Replace your boring battery notifications with **CasYuk**. It’s beautifully designed with a Neo-Brutalist aesthetic and uses hilarious meme alerts to remind you to plug in. Productivity doesn't have to be boring! 💅
