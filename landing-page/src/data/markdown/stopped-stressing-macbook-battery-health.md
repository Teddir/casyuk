# Why I Stopped Stressing Over My MacBook Battery Health

If you have ever anxiously checked your Mac's "Maximum Capacity" percentage in System Settings and cried when it dropped from 100% to 98%, this post is for you. 🥲

## The Toxicity of Battery Anxiety

We pay a lot of money for our laptops (or our parents do, thank you mom!), so naturally, we want to take care of them. But constantly worrying about unplugging it at exactly 80% is exhausting. 

## The Set-It-and-Forget-It Solution

I downloaded **CasYuk** and never looked back. It’s an app that automatically physically stops your laptop from charging past 80%. You just leave your laptop plugged in at your desk, and CasYuk handles the science in the background. 

Now, I can focus on my essays, my Pinterest boards, and my iced coffee, knowing my laptop is perfectly safe. 🫶
