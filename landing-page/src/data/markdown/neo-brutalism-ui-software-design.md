# Neo-Brutalism in UI Design: Why Software is Getting Louder

For the last decade, software design has been dominated by "Corporate Memphis" and sterile minimalism. Soft shadows, rounded corners, and pastel colors. 

But users are getting bored. Enter **Neo-Brutalism**.

## What is Neo-Brutalism?

Characterized by harsh black borders, high-contrast colors (often primary red, blue, and yellow), and raw, unpolished layouts, Neo-Brutalism is a reaction against the overly-sanitized web.

## Why CasYuk Uses It

CasYuk is a battery management app designed to grab your attention. A soft, pastel notification is easily ignored. A harsh, neo-brutalist alert with a screaming meme video? You literally cannot ignore it.

By using aggressive design, we ensure that our software actually achieves its goal: changing user behavior to save battery life.
