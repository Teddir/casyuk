# Cafe Studying 101: How to Never Run Out of Battery at Starbucks

Picture this: you get an iced matcha latte, you find the perfect cozy corner by the window... and then you realize there are no outlets. And your MacBook is at 30%. Panic sets in. ☕️

## How to Maximize Battery on the Go

1. **Low Power Mode**: Turn this on immediately in your Mac settings. It throttles background tasks.
2. **Close Spotify/Apple Music**: I know, studying without music is illegal, but streaming over cafe Wi-Fi drains battery fast. Download your study playlist for offline listening!

## Prevent the Degradation

The main reason laptops die so fast is because the battery has degraded over the years from overcharging. By using a battery health app like **CasYuk** while you are plugged in at your dorm, you preserve the total capacity of your battery. That means when you finally head to the cafe, your 100% is *actually* 100%. Sit wherever you want, bestie! 💖
