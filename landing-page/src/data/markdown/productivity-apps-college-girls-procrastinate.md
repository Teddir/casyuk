# The Best Productivity Apps for College Girls Who Procrastinate

No judgment here—we all procrastinate. But when midterm season hits, you need a digital toolkit to force you into productivity mode. Here are the absolute best apps for the 2026 school year. 📚✨

## 1. Notion (The Aesthetic Brain)
You already know Notion. It’s the ultimate aesthetic database for tracking syllabi, assignments, and life goals.

## 2. Forest (The Focus Tree)
A cute app where you grow virtual trees by *not* touching your phone. If you open Instagram, your tree dies. (Don't kill the tree!)

## 3. CasYuk (The Battery Savior)
Have you ever used "my laptop died" as an excuse to stop studying? Not anymore! CasYuk will literally interrupt your screen with a funny meme video to remind you to plug your charger in before your laptop dies. It’s aggressive, it’s hilarious, and it keeps you in the zone. 🎓
