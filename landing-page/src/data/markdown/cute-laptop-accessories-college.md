# Cute Laptop Accessories Every College Girl Needs This Semester

Your laptop is basically your lifeline in college, so it deserves to look just as cute as your outfits. Here is the ultimate back-to-school tech accessory list for 2026! 🌸

## 1. The Pastel Hard Case & Keyboard Cover
Protect your Mac from scratches while giving it a pop of color. Lavender and Sage Green are the IT colors this semester.

## 2. A Digital Planner for GoodNotes
Stay organized with a cute, hyperlinked digital planner. It’s a lifesaver for tracking assignments.

## 3. CasYuk: The Aesthetic Battery App
Okay, this is software, not hardware, but hear me out! CasYuk is the cutest utility app ever. Instead of boring gray system alerts, it uses fun, chaotic meme videos (yes, including screaming cats) to tell you when to charge your laptop. It also physically protects your battery health. It’s like a tamagotchi for your MacBook! 🐱✨
