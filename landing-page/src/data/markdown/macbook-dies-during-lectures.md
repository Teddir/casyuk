# Why Your MacBook Dies During 3-Hour Lectures (And How to Fix It)

We’ve all been there: you’re in a freezing lecture hall, the professor is finally explaining what’s actually going to be on the exam, and... *your screen goes black.* 😭

## Why is it draining so fast?

- **Chrome Tabs**: Having 40 tabs open for "research" (and online shopping) is draining your RAM and battery.
- **Screen Brightness**: That gorgeous Retina display eats power. Turn it down!
- **Battery Degradation**: If you always leave your laptop plugged in at your dorm, your battery capacity has probably shrunk.

## The Fix

Start using **CasYuk** before the semester starts. It prevents your battery from degrading by limiting the charge to 80% when you are at your desk. Plus, instead of a boring little notification when your battery is low, CasYuk will literally pop up a funny cat meme on your screen to remind you to plug in before you head to class.
