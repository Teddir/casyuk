# Gamifying Productivity: Using Memes to Build Better Habits

We all know we *should* unplug our laptops when they are full, and plug them in before they die. But in the heat of a coding session or a Netflix binge, we forget.

## The Problem with Notifications

Standard OS notifications are designed to be ignorable. They slide in quietly and disappear into a Notification Center graveyard.

## The Meme Solution

CasYuk uses **Pattern Interruption**. When your battery hits a critical threshold, a transparent video of a meme (like a screaming cat or a low-battery warning dance) overtakes your screen.

It's funny, it's startling, and it completely breaks your "notification blindness." By linking a positive emotional response (humor) to a hardware task (plugging in your laptop), you subconsciously build a healthy habit.
