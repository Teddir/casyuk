# Surviving Midterms: Essential Apps for the Aesthetic Student

Midterm season means living at the library, endless cold brew, and staring at your screen for 12 hours straight. Here is how to make your laptop a stress-free zone. 📖☕️

## Focus Mode is Your Best Friend
Turn on macOS Focus Mode to block all text messages and social media notifications. You do not need to know what your ex is doing right now.

## Protect Your Hardware
If you are at the library all day, your laptop is probably plugged in at 100% for 12 hours. This causes major heat and battery degradation. Run **CasYuk** to keep your charge limited to 80% while plugged in. Your battery stays healthy, your laptop stays cool, and you can focus on passing Chemistry! 🧪💅
